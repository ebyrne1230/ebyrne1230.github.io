November 6 2019

Sign-Ups Are Happening Now!

The beginning of the wrestling season is almost here!

Make sure you are registered on family ID. Season starts Monday, November 18th and to be able to practice the wrestlers have to have completed the family ID registration on or before Thursday, November 14th by 3pm.

Also, pay to play has to be made before our athletes can practice, scholarships are available if pay to play is a problem! Please inquire at the athletic office. Go Cougs!

November 1 2019

Mountain View Wrestling families...we need your help!

The Deschutes Mat club kick off tournament is tomorrow. This is a major fundraiser for Mountain View wrestling.

Please check out the Signup Genius and find somewhere to help or donate your time. We are in specific need of concession stand help. Thanks!
